About

Nair Samajam Higher Secondary School , a government aided Higher Secondary School run by the Kottaram Devaswom , Nedumudy, is one of the leading schools in Alappuzha

The Higher Secondary wing that commenced in 1998, prides itself on the qualityof education it imparts. A group of well experienced dedicated teachers, enthusiastic students, a very active and supporting PTA and above all an excellent management put their hands together to raise the school into the realms of greatness.

The excellence of the school speaks for itself through its meritorious achievements in the Higher Secondary Examination. The school has always been on the top chart being the best school in kuttanad achieving a no less than 90% pass and an admirable number of full A+ every year

Our Highlights

  • A high standard of teaching and learning equipped with smart classrooms
  • A highly disciplined and safe environment
  • Family involvement(constant rapport with parents)
  • Social involvement of students through different clubs like the NSS (National Service Scheme)

NSS Unit 52

The NSS unit of NSHSS (unit 52) with a total strength of 100 vounteers under the leadership of a programme officer flourishes actively. The unit is able to uphold the NSS motto 'Not Me But You' by enabling the volunteers to render selfless service through voluntary social service. This unit has also received the best NSS unit award.

Souhrida Club

Souhrida club an innovative programme launched by the Department of Higher Secondary Education aimed at Adolescent Counselling and Health care also functions in our school. The club conducts various counselling classes for both students and their parents

Career Guidance Cell

Career Guidance Cell: A unit of the Career Guidance Cell of the DHSE functions effectively in the school . The unit arranges classes for the students by experts and motivational speakers aiming at personality development and stress management.It also conducts programmes to guide students to select the right courses and career

ASAP

We have a unit of Additional Skill Acquisition Programme(ASAP) in our school. It focusses on skill development in students there by facilitating their employment placements
